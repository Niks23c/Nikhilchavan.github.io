üìà Key KPIs (via DAX)

  • Total Employees ‚Äì COUNT of unique Employee IDs
  • Attrition Rate ‚Äì % of employees who left
  • Average Age ‚Äì Mean age of current workforce
  • Average Monthly Income
-- Total Employees
Total Employees = DISTINCTCOUNT(HR_Data[EmployeeID])

-- Attrition Rate
Attrition Rate = 
DIVIDE(
  CALCULATE(COUNT(HR_Data[Attrition]), HR_Data[Attrition] = "Yes"),
  COUNTROWS(HR_Data)
)

-- Average Age
Average Age = AVERAGE(HR_Data[Age])

-- Avg Monthly Income
Avg Monthly Income = AVERAGE(HR_Data[MonthlyIncome])
