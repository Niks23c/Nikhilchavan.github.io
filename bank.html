🏦 Bank Customer Churn Analysis – Power BI

This Power BI project analyzes factors influencing customer churn in a bank and visualizes trends to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Project Objective

  • Identify customer segments at high risk of churn
  • Uncover behavioral and financial patterns leading to churn
  • Provide actionable insights for customer retention strategies

🔧 Tools & Technologies Used

  • Power BI (Desktop + Service)
  • DAX (for KPIs and custom calculations)
  • CSV dataset import & transformation

🧩 Data Preparation

  • Cleaned columns like gender, tenure, products, balance
  • Converted binary columns to Yes/No
  • Handled missing values and formatted dates

📊 Dashboard Preview

Explore churn metrics and insights using filters by tenure, geography, products, credit score, and more.

🔗 View Dashboard (Power BI Access Required)

📊 Key Metrics & DAX Measures

  • Total Customers
  • Total Churned
  • Churn Rate (%)
  • Average Balance
-- Churn Rate Calculation
CustomerChurn_rate = 
DIVIDE(
  COUNTROWS(FILTER(Customers, Customers[Churn] = "Yes")),
  COUNTROWS(Customers),
  0
)

📈 Dashboard Features

  • Drill-through by tenure, geography, products, and credit score
  • Slicer-based dynamic filters
  • Segment-wise churn visualizations
  • Modern theme and branded tooltips

💡 Key Insights

  • Churn rate is highest among customers with fewer products
  • Low tenure and low balance customers are most likely to churn
  • Specific geographic regions show increased churn risks
  • Clear segmentation allows targeted customer retention strategies
